About US

Sada El-Nafs International Acadimy

Sada Elnafs International Academy was established in 2012 with Contract No. 1225 in accordance with Law 34 of 1976

Our vision
We are looking forward to achieving national leadership in the field of training and development in order to be the first choice for professionals and amateurs and to guide people to their happiness with real science, professional training and high ethics.
Our mission
We look forward to contributing to the development of human resources for institutions and individuals by providing the latest professional training programs and serving sincere advice according to the latest scientific research. We are working to change society as it should be.
Our goals
  • Sada Elnafs International Academy seeks to achieve excellence and leadership and keep pace with the latest training methods
  • We look forward to raising an effective generation that participates in all aspects of life
  • Provide human development to positive energy
  • Spreading a sense of hope among young people
  • Work with government, private and private institutions
  • The academy provides services that aim to develop the necessary skills for employees of various bodies. We also focus on training and qualifying workers in private or official institutions.
  • The academy works to develop the skills of trainers
  • We have a strategic partnership with official private institutions, including Ain Shams University and Harvard Training College
  • We have accreditation from the Faculty of Arts for Girls, Ain Shams University
